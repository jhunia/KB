Refund Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

1. Return Eligibility

You may return items within 7 days of delivery if they meet the following criteria:

  • Items are unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • All original tags and packaging are intact
  • No signs of use, damage, or alteration
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation) is provided

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Underwear and intimate apparel
  • Items with visible signs of wear or damage
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Custom or personalized items

3. Return Process

To initiate a return, follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support at support@kb.ent within 7 days of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Our team will review your request and provide a return authorization
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  5. Ship the item to the address provided in your return authorization

4. Refund Timeline

Once we receive your returned item:

  • We will inspect the item within 2-3 business days
  • If approved, refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds will be credited to your original payment method
  • You will receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed

5. Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to:

  • Defective or damaged item
  • Incorrect item shipped
  • Manufacturing defect

In these cases, we will provide a prepaid shipping label and refund any shipping costs incurred.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Provide photos of the damage, and we will arrange for a replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you.

7. Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size or color, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item.

8. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within 10 business days of approval, please check with your bank or credit card company first. If you still haven't received your refund, contact us at support@kb.ent.
